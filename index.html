Wedding Day Events

Join us at the Denver Athletic Club in downtown Denver, CO on Saturday August 11, 2018 to celebrate our marriage.

5:30 PM: The Ceremony

The ceremony takes place on the rooftop of the Denver Athletic club. Parking is available at the facility for $10 and you are able to leave your vehicle over night if you have a little too much fun. Otherwise, just take an Uber, Lyft, or taxi. Please be there by 5:15 PM.

6:00 PM: Happy Hour

Enjoy drinks with friends and family while snacking on Imported & Domestic Cheeses with Gourmet Crackers and fresh fruit.

7:00 PM: Dinner

Sit down, relax, and enjoy a plated dinner, followed by a donut bar for the sweet tooth in you. The cocktail bar will be closed during dinner, but there will be wine service at the table available for anyone still trying to build courage for the dance floor. Speeches and a champagne toast will get the entertainment going.

8:00 PM: The Reception

The bride & groom will get the dance party going with their first dance and will hope to occupy the floor with everyone. Enjoy an open bar while you dance the night away under the stars and the city lights

11:00 PM: The After Party

Continue the party at the Billiards Room & Pub inside the Denver Athletic Club. There will be music, pizza, cash bar, pool, shuffle board and fun.

1:00 AM: The End

Time to say your good byes and wander back to your bed. Whether you're staying near or far, make sure to plan for a safe ride home. Bars are open until 2:00 AM if you're looking to be the MVP. (If we know you all like we think we do I am sure someone will have a cooler of beers in their hotel room.)
